Why Cape Town?

Cape Town, the Mother City

Legend has it that Cape Town earned the name "The Mother City" as the first European settlement in South Africa, cradled between ocean and mountain. Nestled at the foot of the iconic Table Mountain, the city grew where two great oceans meet, shaped by centuries of culture, trade, and resilience.

Today, beyond the vibrant markets, art-filled streets, and global cuisine, you can still trace the city's soul—from the cobbled lanes of Bo-Kaap to the sweeping views at the mountain’s summit, where land, sea, and sky stretch endlessly into the horizon.

My favorite sites to visit in Cape Town

Table Mountain

Table Mountain is a flat-topped mountain overlooking the city of Cape Town in South Africa. It is a prominent landmark and a popular tourist destination, offering stunning views of the city and the surrounding coastline.

Address:

5821 Tafelberg Rd, Table Mountain (Nature Reserve), Cape Town, 8001

What I like about it

This is one of the most iconic spots in Cape Town, but no matter when you go, the views from the top are always breathtaking!

Bo-Kaap

Bo-Kaap is a vibrant and historic neighborhood in Cape Town, known for its brightly colored houses and cobbled streets. Steeped in Cape Malay culture, it offers a rich blend of history, cuisine, and community, making it one of the most culturally unique and photogenic areas in the city.

Address:

71 Wale Street, Cape Town, 8000

What I like about it

This is one of Cape Town’s most colorful neighborhoods, and no matter when you visit, its charm and culture always shine through!

Learn more

Muizenberg Beach

Muizenberg Beach is a popular seaside destination located along the False Bay coast of Cape Town. Known for its colorful beach huts and gentle waves, it’s a favorite spot for surfers, swimmers, and families, offering a laid-back atmosphere and scenic ocean views

Address:

Beach Rd, Muizenberg, Cape Town, 7950

What I like about it

This is one of the most beloved beaches in Cape Town, and no matter when you go, the colorful huts and rolling waves create the perfect seaside escape!
